## Tips for Choosing the Right Foam & Protective Packaging

- Assess product fragility and identify the most vulnerable points.  
- Choose foam density based on weight and impact tolerance.  
- Combine foam inserts with sturdy outer cartons for stacked shipping.  
- Consider moisture barriers for long-distance or export shipments.  
- Prototype and test with small runs before committing to full production.
